Mission Overview

What is Jiggy By Nature? J.B.N. is you, it is the way you dress yourself and present yourself to others. You don't have to wear the most expensive clothes to be "jiggy" the level of "jiggy-ness" is determined based on your own taste of fashion, how can you take the clothes you have in your cloest and make it look eye catching. The clothes don't define you, you define it. The goal of Jiggy By Nature is to portray that into everyone. The famous artists played a major role into the creation of J.B.N. mainly because they are "jiggy." They don't care what others have to say about the way they dress, they feel comftorable in whatever they where. Be jiggy and be comftorable, wear what you want, be who you want to be, embrace yourself.
